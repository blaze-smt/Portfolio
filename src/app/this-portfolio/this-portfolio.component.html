This Portfolio

Welcome to my portfolio website. I keep track of my creative projects here. It took a lot of trial and error to get to this point, but in the end im very happy with it and I think it represents me well. During the Summer of 2022 I had a personal goal of designing a mobile landing page everyday. I was able to do it for 5 months straight. This experience was good because my skills in mobile design were improved and it also got me more familiar with Figma. The last design I created for that project was a mobile view for this portfolio. Users

I started by determining my target demographic. To do that I made up personas and scenarios for employers, clients, and fellow creatives. This gave me a good idea of the type of content I needed to show on here.

Spec Doc

The Specification Document is where I lay out business goals, website functionality, the site map, 3rd Party APIs, etc... This finalizes the purpose of the website and what I need to achieve that.

Wireframes

When I start my wireframe process I like to hand draw them because its a lot faster and easier to revise. When I feel comfortable with those drawings, I translate them onto Figma. The wireframes give me a good idea of what the mockups/Prototypes should look like.
