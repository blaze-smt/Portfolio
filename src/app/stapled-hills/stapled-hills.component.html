Stapled Hills - Owner

Stapled Hills was my second clothing brand. I saw it as a step up from infst. I wanted Stapled Hills to tell a story and have each piece connect with one another. The brand needed to have a piece of me incorporated into it so all of the designs were based around pixel art because I grew up playing video games. this was also around the time I got into web design. The website was designed by me. This is technically the first site I designed. I will always love Stapled Hills. When it was started I was 20 years old and I really tried hard to make everything for it look good. I worked with professionals to sew on tags, screenprint, clean up my designs. I also purchased higher quality blanks from rue porter and LA Apparel. That was my perception of what a high quality clothing brand included at the time.
