Bohlman Auto Aesthetics

Bohlman Auto Aesthetics is a local Wisconsin business owned by a close friend. I worked with him to make the logo and I sketched it up in Adobe Illustrator. We have plans for a website to be created in Wordpress for mid December, 2022.

Logo Design

The client approached me around October, 2022 asking if I had skills in Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator since he knew I had taken graphic design classes. I asked him why and he said that he needed a professional logo for his business and he was going to do it himself. I offered him my service because I wanted something to put on my portfolio. I created his logo in Illustrator by hand. I worked closely with Colton on specifics of what he was looking for, and I gave him a few variations to choose from.
