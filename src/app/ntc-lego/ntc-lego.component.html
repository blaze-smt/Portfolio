NTC Lego - Front-end Developer / Designer

This was the capstone project for my associates degree at Northcentral Technical College. NTC Lego is a inventory management system used to manage inventory, orders, and sales. My role in this project was Front-end Developer and Designer. Our team consisted of 5 people, 4 software developers and me as the lone web designer. Our instructor Dominic Gruetzmacher held the roles of Product Manager and Client. We worked using Agile methodology and were able to finish the project in 5 sprints. A Stakeholder meeting and 5 report outs were conducted throughout the period of the class. Dominic was able to give us great insight on this application because he owns the biggest lego business in the United States. We had the chance to meet with his personal team that has a real life application extremely similar to this project. Blazor and ASP.Net were utilized as the stack for this application.
